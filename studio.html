01  Our story

We design the moment — then we deliver it.

Lilla Signatures was founded in 2019 in Lower Kabete, Nairobi, on a simple conviction: a room should do more than look beautiful. It should move people toward what you came to say.

We design corporate events and styled celebrations as immersive physical environments — translating brand objectives into spaces guests remember long after the last guest leaves.

The work has earned its way across categories. What began as corporate design now crosses into weddings, milestone birthdays, themed galas and brand activations — because the discipline travels. What never changes is the part you never see: seamless, deadline-driven delivery.

“Design that inspires. Delivery that empowers.”
