Whatever the brief, the build is obsessive and the hand-off is flawless. Here’s what we stage — and how each comes to life.

01 — Corporate Events

Corporate Events

Conferences, AGMs, product launches and internal celebrations, staged with boardroom precision. We translate your objective into a room that does the talking — then run it so your team can focus on the message.

  • Set design & branding
  • Run-sheet management
  • AV & staging
  • Tablescaping
02 — Conferences & Summits

Conferences & Summits

Main-stage builds, branded sets, delegate lounges and registration experiences engineered to run on time. From plenary to networking, every touchpoint reinforces the brand and respects the agenda.

  • Main stage & backdrops
  • Delegate experience
  • Lounges & breakouts
  • Wayfinding
03 — Themed Galas

Themed Galas

Immersive worlds, built to the last prop — from 007 casino nights to decade throwbacks and black-tie awards. We commit to the concept fully, so guests step out of the everyday the moment they arrive.

  • Concept & theming
  • Custom props & builds
  • Lighting & mood
  • Photo moments
04 — Weddings

Weddings

Ceremonies and receptions, from intimate gardens to grand halls — composed, unhurried and unmistakably yours. We hold the details so the two of you can hold the moment.

  • Ceremony & reception design
  • Florals & draping
  • Lighting & ambience
  • Day-of coordination
05 — Milestone Celebrations

Milestone Celebrations

Birthdays, baby showers and the once-in-a-lifetime — backdrops, balloon artistry, marquee numbers and all the feeling. Big or intimate, we make the guest of honour the centre of the room.

  • Backdrops & balloon art
  • Marquee & signage
  • Tablescaping
  • Kids & family setups
06 — Brand Activations & Outdoor

Brand Activations & Outdoor

Launches, red carpets and stretch-tent statements that put your brand at the centre of the moment. We design for the open air and the camera alike — arrivals that announce, spaces that hold a crowd.

  • Stretch tents & structures
  • Red-carpet arrivals
  • Branded environments
  • Outdoor lighting
